About

I am Yubang Wang (王昱邦), a fourth-year undergraduate student in the School of Computer Science at Wuhan University.

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in OpenMOSS Lab, jointly hosted by the College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at Fudan University and the Shanghai Innovation Institute.

Advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu, my research focuses on embodied AI and agents, with an emphasis on agentic embodied AI.

I am especially interested in building and evaluating agents that can perceive, plan, and act in open-ended environments, including research environments where real code execution is the bottleneck.
